About Lending Tree Books

Lending Tree Books was started in 1984, as a used bookstore. Today 30 years later it is still run by Trisha. Lending Tree Books is now a full service bookstore, carrying all the latest hardcovers and paperbacks. We believe in books and the power of the written word to impact lives and societies across the globe, and we cherish our role in saving books to live another day to educate, entertain, inspire, and transform lives.
